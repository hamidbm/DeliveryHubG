## What’s next Commitment Review v1 is a major governance milestone. The next step should ensure the system can drive *continuous* decision-making, not just point-in-time reviews. Right now you have: - Commit reviews as snapshots - Change feed events - Notifications/digests - Program + capacity views But you don’t yet have the executive “control loop” that answers: - Which committed milestones are *drifting* week-over-week? - What changed since last review that reduced our hit probability? - Are we still within the commitment envelope (capacity, quality, critical path, staleness)? - Do we need a re-review or escalation?
