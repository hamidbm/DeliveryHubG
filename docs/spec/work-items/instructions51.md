## What’s next 

Baseline + delta accounting is the last major missing piece for credible drift attribution. The next step is to turn that attribution into a **structured executive narrative** that can be consumed weekly without digging through UI panels. You already have: - Change Feed - Commit reviews and drift snapshots - Baseline deltas - Monte Carlo distribution - Capacity plan - Critical path + graph - Notifications/digests What’s missing is a single artifact that answers: - What changed this week? - Are we more or less likely to hit the date? - Why did confidence change (scope vs velocity vs blockers)? - What are the top actions for next week? So the next step is: ## Next: Weekly Executive Brief v1 (Auto-generated narrative per Program/Bundle/Milestone) This becomes the “Monday morning” output.
