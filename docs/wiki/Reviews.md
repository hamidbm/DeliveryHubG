# Reviews

Reviews are a shared governance capability in DeliveryHub.

They are used to move important documents and architecture artifacts through visible feedback cycles instead of relying on informal review through email, chat, or disconnected meetings.

## Where Reviews Are Used

Reviews are currently used in:

- the **Wiki** module for pages and uploaded assets
- the **Architecture** module for diagrams and architecture artifacts

## What a Review Does

A review cycle captures:

- who is reviewing
- what artifact is under review
- when feedback was requested
- what comments or guidance were provided
- how the owner responded
- when the cycle was closed or restarted

This makes review work visible and traceable.

## Core Review Model

### Cycle-based governance

A single artifact can go through multiple review cycles over time.

This is important because many artifacts require several rounds of feedback before they are truly ready.

### Assigned reviewers

Reviews are not anonymous or ambiguous. Reviewers are explicitly assigned so ownership of the review task is clear.

### Review comments separated from general discussion

DeliveryHub distinguishes between ordinary artifact discussion and review-specific feedback, making it easier to understand which comments were part of the formal governance cycle.

### Visible follow-up in Work Items

Each review request creates linked work so the organization can see that review effort exists and whether it is progressing.

## Typical Roles in a Review

### Requester or owner

The requester submits the artifact for review and responds to the feedback.

### Reviewer

The reviewer provides comments, guidance, and cycle feedback.

### Governance leadership

Admin and governance-aligned roles help ensure reviewer assignment and review discipline remain consistent.

## Why This Capability Matters

Reviews matter because they turn governance into observable work.

Instead of asking whether an artifact was reviewed, DeliveryHub can show:

- the cycle history
- the reviewers
- the feedback
- the follow-up work
- the closure path

## How Reviews Connect to Other Modules

- **Wiki** and **Architecture** are the current review entry points
- **Work Items** tracks visible execution follow-up
- **Activities** and events make review history visible over time
- **Dashboards** and **AI Insights** can incorporate review-related risk and congestion signals

## Recommended Use

Use formal reviews for artifacts that materially affect migration design, delivery timing, compliance, governance, or stakeholder confidence.

That keeps important decisions visible and reduces the risk of hidden review work slowing the program later.
