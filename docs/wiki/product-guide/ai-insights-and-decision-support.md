# AI Insights and Decision Support

This capability is played by the **AI Insights** module.

## What It Means

AI Insights is the decision-support layer of DeliveryHub.

It does not replace human judgment and it does not silently modify business data. Its role is to help users understand the portfolio faster, identify meaningful issues earlier, and explore better decisions with evidence.

## What Is Implemented

### Portfolio Summary

AI Insights can generate a portfolio summary that explains:

- overall health
- top risks
- recommended actions
- concentration signals
- questions leaders should be asking

### Structured Intelligence

The product has moved beyond loose text summaries into a more structured intelligence model. This makes the output easier to trust, compare, and reuse.

### Evidence-backed Q&A

Users can ask portfolio questions in natural language and receive evidence-backed answers rather than generic chat responses.

### Related-entity drill-down

Answers and signals can point back to the work items, milestones, reviews, bundles, and applications behind the conclusion.

### Saved investigations

Users can save important questions and their answers, pin them, refresh them later, and build a repeatable investigation workflow.

### Evidence exploration

Users can inspect the supporting evidence behind an answer instead of treating AI output as a black box.

### Portfolio trend analysis

AI Insights can show whether things are improving or deteriorating over time instead of only showing today’s state.

### Portfolio health scoring

The system can present an overall health score to help leaders gauge the broad delivery posture quickly.

### Alerts and proactive operations intelligence

The product can surface alerts around issues such as:

- worsening delivery trends
- milestone slip risk
- capacity pressure
- review bottlenecks

### Watchers and notifications

Users can subscribe to specific AI-generated signals and receive in-app or external notifications when conditions are triggered.

### Executive Portfolio Summary

The module includes a more executive-focused narrative summary for leadership use.

### Portfolio Forecasting and Predictive Delivery Signals

AI Insights can provide forward-looking forecast signals about likely delivery pressure and upcoming execution concerns.

### Cross-Project Risk Propagation and Dependency Intelligence

This is one of the most important advanced capabilities in the product.

It helps users understand not just where a problem exists, but how that problem could ripple into:

- other bundles
- other milestones
- other applications
- other teams

### Strategic AI Advisor

Leadership can ask high-level portfolio questions and receive grounded, evidence-linked strategic answers and follow-up suggestions.

### AI-Driven Optimization and What-If Scenario Planning

This is another major advanced capability.

Users can explore **what-if scenarios** and strategic alternatives, such as shifts in priorities, staffing, milestone timing, or work allocation, before making a real-world decision.

### Collaborative Workflow Automation

AI Insights can also recommend action plans, suggested tasks, and workflow rules so insight can turn into visible follow-through.

### Action planning

The module can move from diagnosis into suggested next steps so teams do not stop at awareness alone.

## Why These Features Matter

- leaders get faster access to insight without waiting for manual synthesis
- the product supports investigation, not just reporting
- risk becomes easier to explain with linked evidence
- teams can test options before making disruptive portfolio decisions
- AI becomes a practical operating aid rather than a novelty feature

## Important Principle

AI in DeliveryHub is assistive only.

That means:

- humans remain responsible for decisions
- humans choose whether to apply outputs
- AI helps accelerate understanding, prioritization, and scenario analysis

## How Executives Should Use This in Practice

### CIO

Use AI Insights to get a fast evidence-backed read on portfolio health, likely disruption points, and strategic decision options before executive forums.

### VP

Use saved investigations, executive summaries, and propagation analysis to understand where problems are isolated and where they are spreading.

### PMO

Use AI-generated alerts, trends, and action plans to focus operating reviews on the few issues that most need follow-through.

### Architects

Use AI explanations and related-entity drill-down to investigate why a dependency, review bottleneck, or architecture concern is surfacing at portfolio level.

### Delivery managers

Use scenario planning and task suggestions to test options quickly, then explicitly decide which actions to apply in the real plan.
